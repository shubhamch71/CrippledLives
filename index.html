Crippled Lives

Disability is just a term


Never ignore somebody with a disability, you don't realize how much they can inspire you!!! You can help kids with disabilities enjoy full lives. Having a disability shouldn’t mean being excluded. Over 1 billion people around the world have some form of disability — many of them children. Whether affected by disease, land mines, or other injuries, children with disabilities in developing countries often find themselves isolated, lonely, and with little hope.
